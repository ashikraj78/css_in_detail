What is Lorem Ipsum?

Why do we use it ?

In publishing and graphic design, lorem ipsum is a filler text commonly used to demonstrate the graphic elements of a document or visual presentation. Replacing meaningful content that could be distracting with placeholder text may allow viewers to focus on graphic aspects such as font, typography, and page layout. You might wonder why a designer would choose to use lorem ipsum text instead of a few paragraphs in English or their native language. Why not just copy and paste a page from an old book or something?The main reason for this is to keep people from focusing on the actual text. When someone creates a template (or uses a free template website) and wants to get feedback on it, they don’t want the people reviewing it to get distracted by what the text says.Additionally, lorem ipsum closely resembles actual text. Copying and pasting the same few words would result in an uneven distribution of letters.
